Smart Classroom & Timetable Scheduler

An intelligent web-based platform that optimizes class scheduling for higher education institutions, maximizing resource utilization and minimizing conflicts.

Key Features

Optimized Scheduling

Intelligent algorithm that considers faculty availability, classroom capacity, and student preferences to create conflict-free timetables.

Multi-Department Support

Schedule classes across multiple departments with shared resources and varying academic calendars.

Dynamic Adjustments

Real-time adjustments for faculty leaves, room unavailability, and last-minute changes with automatic reoptimization.

Resource Analytics

Detailed reports on classroom utilization, faculty workload distribution, and optimization metrics.

Secure Access

Role-based access control with secure login for administrators, faculty, and department heads.

Mobile Friendly

Responsive design that works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Scheduling Parameters

Classroom Resources

Number of available classrooms and laboratories with their respective capacities and equipment.

Faculty Availability

Teaching load limits, preferred time slots, and average monthly leave patterns for each instructor.

Student Requirements

Batch sizes, elective course selections, and special accommodation needs.

Curriculum Constraints

Subject prerequisites, credit hour requirements, and examination scheduling considerations.

Time Constraints

Lunch breaks, assembly periods, and fixed-slot requirements for special classes.

Optimization Goals

Prioritization of objectives such as workload balance, resource utilization, and student satisfaction.
